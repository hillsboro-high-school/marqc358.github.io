    • The Denver Nuggets: #2 in the west

    The Denver Nuggets have had a great regular season with a record of 54-28 which places them #2 in the west this regular season. They've made it to the second round in the playoffs but were eliminated by the Portland Trail Blazers in a record of 4-3. During game 3 with the Blazers, history was made as both teams played very hard and ended up going to 4 overtimes, although they lost 140-137! This has only happened one other time in the NBA playoffs in 1952-1953 when the Boston Celtics and the Syracuse Nationals went against eachother in the Eastern Division Semifinals.

    The Denver Nuggets are a team based in Denver, Colorado. It was founded in 1967. They have qualified for the playoffs many times but have yet to be able to play in the championship round.
