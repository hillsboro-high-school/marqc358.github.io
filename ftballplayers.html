    • The Toronto Raptors: #2 in the east

    The Toronto Raptors are the 2nd best team in the east according to their record in the regular season, which was 58-24, right behind the Milwaukee Bucks who had a record of 60-22. Although bested by the bucks in the regular season, the Raptors managed to beat them in the playoffs with a series record of 4-2. They are currently in the finals up agaisnt the Golden State Warriors, the #1 team for the west. They were arguably able to enter the finals thanks to Kawhi Leonard who hit an outstanding game winning shot in game 7 against the 76ers. Although this was only in the 2nd round of the playoffs, it was still a crucial shot to make if they wanted to move up. The Raptors vs Warriors series right now is tied at 1-1.

    The Raptors are the only professional Canadian basketball team in the NBA. They were founded in 1995 as part of the NBA's expansion into Canada. They struggled in their early years but were then led later on by Vince Carter in 2001 where he led them to their first playoff series win. Currently, they have Kawhi Leonard, another amazing player, who has led them into the finals this year against the warriors.
